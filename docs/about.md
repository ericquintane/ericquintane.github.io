  <section class="detail-card detail-card--wide">
    <h2>Research profile</h2>
    <p>
      My research focuses on the dynamics of interpersonal networks and their
      consequences for individuals and organizations. I study how relationships
      shape outcomes such as creativity, collaboration, learning, and burnout,
      drawing on perspectives from organizational behavior, social networks, and
      social capital.
    </p>
    <p>
      This work has been published in peer-reviewed journals across management,
      psychology, and sociology. Much of it looks at the tension between the
      structures people create, the structures in which they perform best, and
      the behavioral processes that unfold between those two realities.
    </p>
